Abbott Elementary

Abbott Elementary is a comedy created by Quinta Brunson. It follows a the staff of a public elementary school in Philadelphia in a mockumentary style. The show has been running since December of 2021 and airs on ABC.

The show has won multiple Emmy awards. Quinta Brunson both writes for and stars in the show. The staff of Abbott is being filmed for a documentary at the request of the school's principal. The main staff's personal and professional lives are followed, along with various side characters.

  • Quinta Brunson plays Janine Teagues
  • Tyler James Williams plays Gregory Eddie
  • Lisa Ann Walter plays Melissa Schemmenti
  • Sheryl Lee Ralph plays Barbara Howard
  • Chris Perfetti plays Jacob Hill
  • Janelle James plays Ava Coleman
  • William Stanford Davis plays Mr. Johnson
